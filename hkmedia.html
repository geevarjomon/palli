HOLY KINGS MEDIA (HK MEDIA)

HK Media is the dedicated media and communications team of Piravom Valiyapalli, committed to delivering high-quality digital coverage for all church events and activities. As the official media wing of Holy Kings Media, the team specializes in live streaming, event documentation, photography, videography, and content production, ensuring that every significant moment is captured and shared with clarity and professionalism.
