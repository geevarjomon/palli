Privacy Policy

Data Collected

When submitting a nercha purchase request, the website may collect personal details including your name, phone number, and address.

Usage of Data

Collected data is used strictly to process offerings and to contact users only when required for service-related communication.

Data Protection

Personal information is not sold, rented, or shared with unrelated third parties. Data is used only for internal church administrative and religious service purposes.

Payment Security

Payments are processed securely through trusted third-party gateways such as Easebuzz and Razorpay. This website does not store sensitive financial details, including card numbers or banking credentials.

Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies and related browser storage for core functionality and session continuity.

User Rights

Users may contact the church administration for concerns related to personal data, correction requests, or privacy clarifications.
